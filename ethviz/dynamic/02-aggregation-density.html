Density binning

The same transactions.csv, but every transfer endpoint is binned into a screen-space grid instead of drawn individually. This is a scale technique: at 100 points it's just showing the mechanism — the payoff is at millions, where individual marks are sub-pixel. World-space binning (HexagonLayer) is the closer analog to your pre-baked LOD tiles. Grow the CSV to feel it bite.
