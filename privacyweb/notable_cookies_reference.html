Comprehensive guide to cookies commonly found in privacy investigations

About This Guide

This reference guide documents notable cookies frequently discovered during website privacy investigations. Each entry includes the cookie's name, affiliated company, purpose, typical lifespan, and privacy implications.

Cookies are organized by category (Analytics, Advertising, Security, etc.) and include risk assessments based on privacy impact. Use this guide to quickly identify and understand cookies found in your Blacklight privacy reports.

Risk Level Legend

High Risk Extensive tracking/profiling
Medium Risk Moderate tracking capabilities
Low Risk Essential or minimal tracking

üìä Analytics & Measurement Cookies

Track user behavior, page views, and website performance metrics

_ga
Google Analytics Third-Party High Risk
Primary Google Analytics tracking cookie that assigns a unique identifier to website visitors. This cookie tracks users across multiple sessions and is used to distinguish unique visitors.

Technical Details:

  • Typical Expiration: 2 years
  • Domain: Website domain or .google-analytics.com
  • Data Collected: User ID, session data, page views, referrer information
  • Shared With: Google advertising network, Google partners
Privacy Impact: Enables long-term user tracking across multiple visits. Data may be linked to Google advertising profiles if user is signed into Google services. Can track users across different websites using Google Analytics.
_gid
Google Analytics Third-Party Medium Risk
Short-term Google Analytics identifier used to distinguish users during a 24-hour period. Works in conjunction with _ga for more granular tracking.

Technical Details:

  • Typical Expiration: 24 hours
  • Domain: Website domain
  • Data Collected: Temporary user ID, session interactions
  • Purpose: Track user behavior within a single day
_gat
Google Analytics Third-Party Low Risk
Google Analytics throttle cookie used to limit the rate of data collection requests to their servers. Primarily technical rather than tracking-focused.

Technical Details:

  • Typical Expiration: 1 minute
  • Domain: Website domain
  • Purpose: Request rate limiting
_gat_gtag_UA_*
Google Analytics Third-Party Low Risk
Property-specific Google Analytics throttle cookie. The asterisk (*) is replaced with the unique property ID (e.g., UA-123456-1).

Technical Details:

  • Typical Expiration: 1 minute
  • Purpose: Rate limiting per property
s_vi
Adobe Analytics (Omniture) Third-Party High Risk
Adobe Analytics visitor identifier that tracks unique visitors across sessions and time periods. Part of Adobe's Experience Cloud tracking ecosystem.

Technical Details:

  • Typical Expiration: 2-5 years
  • Domain: .2o7.net or website domain
  • Data Collected: Unique visitor ID, visit patterns, behavior tracking
  • Shared With: Adobe Experience Cloud, client company
Privacy Impact: Very long persistence allows tracking over years. Data shared with Adobe's extensive advertising and analytics network.
s_cc
Adobe Analytics Third-Party Low Risk
Adobe Analytics cookie that simply detects whether cookies are enabled in the user's browser. Contains only the value "true".

Technical Details:

  • Typical Expiration: Session
  • Purpose: Cookie capability detection

üì¢ Advertising & Marketing Cookies

Enable targeted advertising and cross-site user tracking

_fbp
Facebook (Meta) Third-Party High Risk
Facebook Pixel browser cookie that stores and tracks visits across websites with Facebook Pixel installed. Primary cookie for Facebook's cross-site tracking.

Technical Details:

  • Typical Expiration: 90 days
  • Domain: Website domain
  • Data Collected: Page views, events, conversions, user behavior
  • Shared With: Facebook/Meta advertising platform, Instagram
Privacy Impact: Tracks users across all websites with Facebook Pixel. Links browsing behavior to Facebook profiles even if not logged in. Used for behavioral ad targeting.
fr
Facebook (Meta) Third-Party High Risk
Facebook's primary advertising cookie used to deliver, measure, and improve the relevancy of ads both on and off Facebook.

Technical Details:

  • Typical Expiration: 90 days
  • Domain: .facebook.com
  • Data Collected: Browser ID, ad delivery data, user behavior
  • Purpose: Ad targeting, measurement, optimization
Privacy Impact: Central to Facebook's advertising surveillance infrastructure. Enables detailed behavioral profiling and retargeting across the web.
IDE
Google DoubleClick Third-Party High Risk
Google DoubleClick cookie used to track users across websites and serve targeted advertisements. Core component of Google's advertising network.

Technical Details:

  • Typical Expiration: 13 months
  • Domain: .doubleclick.net
  • Data Collected: User ID, browsing patterns, ad interactions, demographics
  • Shared With: Google Ads network, thousands of partner websites
Privacy Impact: Tracks users across millions of websites in Google's advertising network. Builds comprehensive behavioral profiles for targeted advertising.
test_cookie
Google DoubleClick Third-Party Low Risk
Short-lived cookie used by DoubleClick to check whether the browser supports cookies. Despite the name, still used for tracking infrastructure.

Technical Details:

  • Typical Expiration: 15 minutes
  • Domain: .doubleclick.net
  • Purpose: Cookie capability testing
_gcl_au
Google AdSense Third-Party High Risk
Google AdSense conversion tracking cookie that stores information about how users arrived at the site and conversion data.

Technical Details:

  • Typical Expiration: 90 days
  • Domain: Website domain
  • Data Collected: Click IDs, conversion data, attribution information
  • Purpose: Ad conversion tracking and attribution
Privacy Impact: Links ad clicks to on-site behavior and conversions. Data shared with Google's advertising ecosystem.

üõ°Ô∏è Security & Bot Management Cookies

Detect bots, prevent fraud, and manage security challenges

_abck
Akamai Bot Manager Third-Party Medium Risk
Akamai's bot detection cookie that monitors user behavior to distinguish between humans and automated bots. Widely used for security purposes.

Technical Details:

  • Typical Expiration: 1 year
  • Domain: Website domain
  • Data Collected: Mouse movements, keystrokes, timing patterns, device fingerprint
  • Purpose: Bot detection and fraud prevention
Privacy Impact: While used for security, collects detailed behavioral data that could be used for fingerprinting. Long persistence period.
bm_sz
Akamai Bot Manager Third-Party Low Risk
Akamai cookie that collects data about page performance and user behavior timing for bot detection analysis.

Technical Details:

  • Typical Expiration: 2-4 hours
  • Domain: Website domain
  • Purpose: Behavioral analysis for bot detection
ak_bmsc
Akamai Bot Manager Third-Party Medium Risk
Akamai Bot Manager Security Cookie that distinguishes between humans and bots. Part of Akamai's security infrastructure.

Technical Details:

  • Typical Expiration: 2 hours
  • Domain: Website domain
  • Data Collected: Browser fingerprint, behavioral patterns
__cf_bm
Cloudflare Third-Party Low Risk
Cloudflare Bot Management cookie used to distinguish legitimate users from malicious bots. Essential for Cloudflare's security services.

Technical Details:

  • Typical Expiration: 30 minutes
  • Domain: Website domain
  • Purpose: Bot detection, DDoS protection
Privacy Impact: Short-lived and security-focused, minimal privacy concerns. Generally considered essential for site protection.

‚öôÔ∏è Session & Functionality Cookies

Maintain user sessions and website functionality

JSESSIONID
Various (Java-based sites) First-Party Low Risk
Generic session cookie used by Java-based web applications to maintain user session state. Commonly found on e-commerce and enterprise sites.

Technical Details:

  • Typical Expiration: Session (deleted when browser closes)
  • Domain: Website domain
  • Purpose: Session management, login state
  • Data Stored: Random session identifier
Privacy Impact: Minimal - essential for site functionality. Session-only duration limits tracking capability.
PHPSESSID
Various (PHP-based sites) First-Party Low Risk
Default session cookie for PHP applications. Maintains user session and state information during a single browsing session.

Technical Details:

  • Typical Expiration: Session
  • Domain: Website domain
  • Purpose: Session tracking for PHP applications
ASP.NET_SessionId
Various (ASP.NET sites) First-Party Low Risk
Session identifier for ASP.NET applications. Standard Microsoft web framework session cookie.

Technical Details:

  • Typical Expiration: Session
  • Domain: Website domain
  • Purpose: ASP.NET session management

üõí E-Commerce & Shopping Cookies

Shopping cart, preferences, and purchase tracking

dbidv2
Lowe's First-Party Medium Risk
Lowe's database identifier version 2. Persistent tracking cookie used to identify users across sessions on Lowe's website. Tracks shopping behavior and preferences.

Technical Details:

  • Typical Expiration: 2 years
  • Domain: lowes.com
  • Data Collected: User identifier, session tracking, behavioral data
  • Purpose: User identification and tracking across visits
  • Secure Flag: No (can be transmitted over unencrypted connections)
Privacy Impact: Long 2-year persistence enables extended user tracking. Lack of Secure flag means this identifier can be intercepted over HTTP connections, potentially exposing user shopping patterns.
bm_ss
Akamai Bot Manager First-Party (via Lowe's) Low Risk
Akamai Bot Manager session cookie used on Lowe's for bot detection. Part of the security infrastructure to prevent automated scraping and attacks.

Technical Details:

  • Typical Expiration: 1 day
  • Domain: lowes.com
  • Data Collected: Behavioral timing data, interaction patterns
  • Purpose: Bot detection and security
akavpau_default
Akamai First-Party Low Risk
Akamai Virtual Private Audience User cookie. Used by Akamai's CDN for traffic management and performance optimization. On Lowe's, this cookie has no expiration.

Technical Details:

  • Typical Expiration: No expiration (persistent indefinitely)
  • Domain: www.lowes.com
  • Data Collected: User routing data, CDN performance metrics
  • Purpose: CDN traffic optimization
Privacy Impact: No expiration date means this cookie persists indefinitely until manually deleted. While primarily for performance, can enable long-term user tracking.
seo-partner
Lowe's First-Party Medium Risk
Lowe's SEO partner tracking cookie that identifies traffic sources from affiliate and SEO partnerships. Used to track marketing attribution.

Technical Details:

  • Typical Expiration: 1 day
  • Domain: www.lowes.com
  • Data Collected: Referrer source, partner ID, campaign tracking
  • Purpose: Marketing attribution and partner commission tracking
Privacy Impact: Tracks how users arrive at Lowe's website through affiliate links and SEO partnerships. Data may be shared with marketing partners.
E1EXP
Lowe's First-Party Low Risk
Lowe's E1 Experience cookie. Likely used for A/B testing or personalization experiments to optimize user experience and website layouts.

Technical Details:

  • Typical Expiration: 1 week
  • Domain: www.lowes.com
  • Data Collected: Experiment group assignment, feature flags
  • Purpose: A/B testing and experience personalization
region
Lowe's First-Party Low Risk
Lowe's regional preference cookie that stores user's geographic location preference for showing relevant products, pricing, and store availability.

Technical Details:

  • Typical Expiration: 1 day
  • Domain: www.lowes.com
  • Data Collected: Geographic region/store preference
  • Purpose: Localization and inventory display
Privacy Impact: Minimal - primarily functional for showing relevant local information. Can reveal approximate user location.
AKA_A2
Akamai First-Party Low Risk
Akamai acceleration cookie used for HTTP/2 server push optimization. Technical cookie for CDN performance enhancement.

Technical Details:

  • Typical Expiration: 1 hour
  • Domain: lowes.com
  • Purpose: CDN performance optimization
cart
Various E-commerce First-Party Low Risk
Generic shopping cart cookie that maintains items added to cart across sessions. Essential for e-commerce functionality.

Technical Details:

  • Typical Expiration: 2 weeks to 30 days
  • Domain: Website domain
  • Data Stored: Cart ID, selected items
  • Purpose: Shopping cart persistence
Privacy Impact: Minimal when first-party. Essential for shopping functionality. Can reveal shopping interests if intercepted.
Also known as: shopping_cart, basket, cart_id, CART

üìß Email Marketing & Ad Tech Cookies

Email-based advertising and retargeting cookies

lidid
LiveIntent (liadm.com) Third-Party High Risk
LiveIntent Device Identifier. Primary tracking cookie for LiveIntent's email-based advertising platform. Connects email newsletter activity with web browsing behavior for targeted advertising.

Technical Details:

  • Typical Expiration: 2 years
  • Domain: liadm.com (LiveIntent Ad Manager)
  • Data Collected: Email engagement, web visits, ad interactions, behavioral profile
  • Shared With: LiveIntent advertising partners, brand advertisers
  • Secure Flag: No (vulnerable to interception)
Privacy Impact: Highly invasive - links email newsletter activity to web browsing. Creates comprehensive user profiles across email and web. 2-year lifespan enables long-term tracking. No Secure flag increases vulnerability.
_li_ss
LiveIntent Third-Party High Risk
LiveIntent session sync cookie. Used to synchronize user identifiers across different websites and email platforms for cross-site tracking and ad targeting.

Technical Details:

  • Typical Expiration: 30 days
  • Domain: i.liadm.com (LiveIntent infrastructure)
  • Data Collected: User ID synchronization, cross-site tracking data
  • Purpose: Identity resolution across platforms
  • Secure Flag: No
Privacy Impact: Enables LiveIntent to track users across multiple websites and link browsing behavior to email engagement. Part of sophisticated cross-platform tracking infrastructure.
X-AB
Salesforce (sc-static.net) Third-Party Medium Risk
Salesforce Marketing Cloud A/B testing cookie. Used for multivariate testing and content personalization experiments through Salesforce's marketing automation platform.

Technical Details:

  • Typical Expiration: 1 day
  • Domain: sc-static.net (Salesforce Content Delivery)
  • Data Collected: Test group assignment, experiment participation
  • Purpose: A/B testing and marketing optimization
  • Secure Flag: No
Privacy Impact: While primarily for testing, enables behavioral profiling through Salesforce's marketing cloud. Data may be combined with other Salesforce tracking systems.
